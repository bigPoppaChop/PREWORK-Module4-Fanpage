Biography

In the year 2688, humankind has built a Utopia because of the music of Wyld Stallyns.

In the year 1989 however, Wyld Stallyns is known only as two high school slackers named Bill S. Preston, Esq. and Theodore Logan.
The pair have been recently assigned to make a presentation for their history class, which neither has been doing well in.
With a looming threat of military school for Ted if he fails the class and friction between Bill and his mother, the pair begin working on their report
by the Circle K, where strange things are destined to be afoot.

It is here they meet Rufus, a time traveler from the Utopian a who is tasked with helping Bill and Ted with their history presentation.
With some convincing from themselves to trust Rufus, they begin an adventure through time where they aquire the assistance of some of history's most notable people.
After saving the historical figures from being locked up for their hijinks at the San Dimas Mall, Bill and Ted give their presentation and pass their course.
